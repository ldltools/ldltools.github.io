    dsl4sc

    DSL4SC is a small experimental domain-specific language for defining correct smart contracts that are guaranteed to meet their formally-specified requirements.

    Commands

    rulessat

    usage: rulessat <contract>.rules

    rulessat solves the satisfiability of the input DSL4SC contract included in <contract>.rules and returns either valid, satisfiable, or unsatisfiable.

    rulesmc

    usage: rulesmc -m <model>.rules <property>.rules

    Given a model and a property contract in DSL4SC, rulesmc checks whether model ‚ä® property holds or not.

    rules2scxml

    usage: rules2scxml <contract>.rules

    rules2scxml translates the input DSL4SC contract into an equivalent SCXML representation.
